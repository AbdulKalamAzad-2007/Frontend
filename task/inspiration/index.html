Early Life and Rise in Kabaddi

Coming from a family deeply rooted in kabaddi, Sudhakar was inspired by his father and elder brothers who played the sport. Despite initial restrictions on traveling for tournaments, a chance opportunity in 11th standard marked a turning point in his career. His dedication and self-training in the muddy fields paid off when he emerged as the best raider in the 2022 Winter Edition of the Yuva Kabaddi Series, scoring 320 raid points with a strike rate of 77.1%.
